ROI Estimator FAQs

How accurate is this estimate?

It’s a directional ROI model based on your selections and typical conditions. Final pricing depends on window size, style, installation conditions, and any structural work.

Where does the resale value % come from?

We use a ~70% recoup assumption informed by national cost-vs-value trends for window projects. It is a planning guide—your local market may vary.

How is energy savings calculated?

We start with a baseline $250 monthly bill (editable in the drawer), apply a rough regional inflation rate derived from your ZIP’s first digit, and multiply by the selected package’s efficiency over the years you plan to stay.

How is ROI calculated?

ROI = Energy Savings + Resale Value − Installed Cost + Discount. Discounts reduce out-of-pocket for the same product value, so ROI increases; they don’t reduce Savings or Resale.
